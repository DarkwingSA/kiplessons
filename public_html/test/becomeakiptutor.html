Become a Kip McGrath Tutor

We are always looking for Great, Effective and Passionate Tutors

If you can answer YES to ALL of the questions below, then you may be what we are looking for:

  1. Do you have a passion to teach children?
  2. Do you have your own transport?
  3. Do you have teaching qualifications in English, Mathematics, or Afrikaans?
  4. Did you have a good Matric mark in English, Mathematics, or Afrikaans?
  5. Is English or Afrikaans your first language?
  6. Do you have any teaching / tutor experience?
  7. Are you available at least 2 afternoons a week?
If you would like to apply for a position as a tutor, please e-mail your Curriculum Vitae, certified copies of your qualifications and police clearance certificate plus a photo of yourself to the relevant centre you are interested in working for. See our centre locator to find your nearest centre.

Note: If you have not received any response within 30 days, please consider the application unsuccessful and try again at a later stage.
