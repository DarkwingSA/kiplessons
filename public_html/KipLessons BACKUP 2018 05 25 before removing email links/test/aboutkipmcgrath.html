About Kip McGrath

We are champions for children's education

The first Kip McGrath Education Centre opened its doors in Newcastle NSW 40 years ago with the founding principle, "any child can learn if they are taught properly". Founded by school tutors Kip and Dug McGrath, that first tutoring centre has grown since then into a global franchise network of over 500 tutoring centres owned and operated by qualified and experienced tutors, with a common motivation; a passion for teaching.

Our Philosophy:

  • We know that unresolved gaps in foundational learning can have lifelong impacts on your child.
  • We believe in building confidence, self-esteem and providing results for your child.
  • We believe that all children can succeed if given the right support. 
  • We think learning can be fun!

"At KMEC, we aim to complement the work being done in schools so that students can find success there too.
There is a definite need and our centres help to fulfil that need." - Founder, Kip McGrath

At our Kip McGrath Education Centres we offer face-to-face tutoring to children from Grade R through to Grade 12.

About our lessons:

  • 80 minute tutoring sessions - designed for a child with concentration problems to cope. 10 minute tasks are broken up, by rotation, so concentration is broken after each task.
  • Small groups (up to 6 students per teacher) - there is no group teaching. The program is individualised for your child. We focus on your child's weaknesses.
  • We have structured learning programmes. English and Afrikaans are broken up into reading, grammar, comprehension, spelling, creative writing and phonemic awareness.
  • Maths is broken up into tables and bonds, problem solving and Maths concepts needed.
  • Our programmes are suitable for all students ranging from those who may be experiencing difficulties in their day to day school work, right through to students who would like to extend their learning ability and achieve improved results.
  • Computer assisted learning - 2 out of 6 tasks on the computer. Enhances the audio aspect of reading.
  • Curriculum written by qualified tutors - aligned to our national curriculum so yes we will follow CAPS curriculum. We follow the IEB Curriculum as well.

The Kip McGrath Difference:

  • Individual learning programmes to cater to your child's needs. Each session includes written and computer-based activities, plus ongoing progress reports and regular tutor feedback.
  • A FREE assessment is a no-obligation educational evaluation, conducted by a qualified teacher. Based on the results, a tailored programme will be created to address your child's learning.
  • Qualified teachers, using proven methods and techniques, will bring out the best in your child from Grade R to Grade 12 level. Trust the experts to bring the fun back into learning.
  • With a 40 year history and an international reputation, Kip McGrath knows what works. Boost your child's success, confidence and self-esteem in Maths and English today.
