Where does this Forum originate and why does it exist?


This forum grew out of an initiative to find an effective way to share the costs of marketing initiatives. i.e. those who pay derive the benefit and there are no "hangers on" who get leads without contributing. The initial solution was to create KipLessons.co.za which is a map of each province with the contributing centres listed. In addition, an extensive Google My Business update process was undertaken to ensure that the biggest search engine knows we are ONE GROUP - and improves their ability to include us in search results. An Adwords campaign was also created to ensure we are found when people search for "extra lessons" (and several other keywords). We also started a Facebook ad campaign to reach people who aren't searching (yet). More recently, sponsorship of focused and select expos and conferences has been included. On this forum we can discuss anything that is relevant to making our business lives easier (and more profitable), but primarily related to:

  • Brand building activities;
  • KipLessons.co.za website;
  • Google Adwords; and
  • Facebook and social media marketing.

Though other matters like the following may also arise :

  • Discuss issues and possible solutions to problems that we might encounter. (Franchise related or business related)
  • Brainstorm marketing ideas.
  • Go to Head Office with suggestions as a forum.
  • We will have buying power for special deals like furniture, stationery etc.
