Directory

WhatsApp

Purpose : For day-to-day communication amongst participating Centres.

Dropbox


Purpose : For sharing of large files, logos, fonts, pictures etc.
Requirements/Action : Click on the logo on the left to access the Dropbox folder. To add content to this folder, please forward it to Tracy-Lee or Paul. If you want to be a regular contributor, please request to be made an additional admin of the folder.

Facebook


Purpose : To have one very popular Facebook Page that we all use for posting inspiration stories, quotes, tips etc. for use by the public. This will eliminate the current "clutter" and confusion. Get your customers to follow, like and share posts as well as everyone you know. This is great for Google rankings.
Requirements/Action : Please send a "friend request" to Paul Albrecht or Tracy-Lee Sydney-Smith so they can add you as a contributor. Then hide or unpublish your own Facebook Page (if you have LOTS of followers already, let us know so we can request Facebook to merge your page into this one)

Website


Purpose : In all KipLessons.co.za search "bubbles" there is a link to YOUR website. If you do not have your own website, the link will take you to your page on the Head Office site. Click the icon on the left to see. Google AdWords will all be driven towards this webpage, do not compete with fellow franchises on the same words.
Requirements/Action : Either email gavin@kiplessons.co.za your own website URL or contact Head Office and ensure that your page on their site is up-to-date and meaningful. View the Google Groups for words that are currently being used and suggest new ones.

Email


Purpose : To standardise email addresses used for contacting a Centre (we currently have so many messy variations). So, when someone finds your Centre on the website, they can email you by clicking on yourcentrename@KipLessons.co.za. This is simply a "forwarded address" that goes to as many of your existing email addresses as you want.
Requirements/Action : Send a mail to Gavin@KipLessons.co.za advising 1) your Centre Name and 2) the list of addresses you want mails to be forwarded to.
