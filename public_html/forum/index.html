Welcome to the Kip Lessons Forum

If you have any questions or queries, do not hesitate to contact Paul on 082 611 1973. Please remember that by joining this forum, you agree to participate in the entire 2017 budget for social media and other brand-building functions, and agree to pay R600 per month for 10 months from beginning January to October. This is not a "drop-in" and "drop-out" plan, but rather a medium- to long-term strategy that builds our brand, improves search relevance and enhances overall credibility (and brings customers!). The WhatsApp Group and this collaborative forum are for contributing members of KipLessons only. This is an entirely voluntary, franchisee-led initiative and not a Head Office programme.

This forum grew out of an initiative to find an effective way to share the costs of marketing initiatives (i.e. those who pay derive the benefit and there are no "hangers-on" who get leads without contributing). The initial solution was to create KipLessons.co.za, which is a map of each province with the contributing centres listed. In addition, an extensive Google My Business update process was undertaken to ensure that the biggest search engine knows we are ONE GROUP – and improves their ability to include us in search results. An Adwords campaign was also created to ensure we are found when people search for "extra lessons" (and several other keywords). We also started a Facebook ad campaign to reach people who aren't searching (yet). More recently, sponsorship of focused and select expos and conferences has been included. On this forum we can discuss anything that is relevant to making our business lives easier (and more profitable), but primarily related to:

  • Brand building activities
  • KipLessons.co.za website
  • Google Adwords
  • Facebook and social media marketing

Though other matters like the following may also arise:

  • Discuss issues and possible solutions to problems that we might encounter (franchise- or business-related)
  • Brainstorm marketing ideas
  • Go to Head Office with suggestions as a forum
  • We will have buying power for special deals like furniture, stationery, etc.

Website

In all KipLessons.co.za search bubbles there is a link to your website. If you do not have your own website, the link will go to your page on the Head Office site. Google AdWords are all driven to this webpage – do not compete with fellow franchises on the same words. Either email Gavin Kennedy your own website URL or contact Head Office and ensure that your page on their site is up-to-date and meaningful. View the Google Groups for words that are currently being used and suggest new ones.

Email

Standardised email addresses used for contacting a centre (we currently have so many messy variations). When someone finds your centre on the website, they can email you by clicking on yourcentrename@KipLessons.co.za. This is simply a forwarded address that goes to as many of your existing email addresses as you want. Email Gavin Kennedy advising your centre name and the list of addresses you want mails to be forwarded to. Standardised (and attractive) email signatures for all are on the agenda for 2017 to provide uniformity of brand and message.
